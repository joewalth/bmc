Bmc Team Member Contract

MEMORANDUM OF AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made on this , by and between Bmc (hereinafter referred to as the "Company"), and all Bmc team members (hereinafter referred to as the "Team Member").

WHEREAS, the Company is engaged in Saas and digital marketing and wishes to retain the services of the Team Member under the terms and conditions set forth herein;

AND WHEREAS, the Team Member agrees to serve the Company and adhere to its policies as detailed below;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. Confidentiality and Use of Knowledge/Resources
The Team Member agrees that all knowledge, resources, and information obtained during their tenure with Bmc shall remain confidential and proprietary to Bmc. The Team Member shall not use or disclose such knowledge, resources, or information for any purpose outside of their duties at Bmc. This obligation shall survive the termination of this Agreement.

2. Company Shares
The Company agrees to allocate 15% of its shares to the Team Members. The Team Member acknowledges and agrees to this allocation and understands that the distribution and specific terms of the shareholding will be governed by the Company's internal policies and any applicable legal requirements.

3. Work Policy
The Team Member agrees to adhere to the following work policies:

  • The Team Member must be active and post daily on the Bmc App or website.
  • The Team Member must promote Bmc via their social media platforms regularly.
  • Failure to be active in their duties without a reasonable and communicated reason may result in their removal as a Team Member at any time at the discretion of the Company.

4. Review of Contract
This contract shall be reviewed every six (6) months. Any amendments or changes to the terms of this Agreement will be discussed and agreed upon during these reviews.

5. Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time, with or without cause, by providing written notice to the other party. Upon termination, the Team Member shall return all Company property and confidential information in their possession.

6. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Nigeria.

7. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations, whether written or oral, regarding the subject matter herein.

8. Severability
If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, such provision shall be struck and the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

9. Acknowledgment
The Team Member acknowledges that they have read and understood the terms of this Agreement and agree to be bound by them.
